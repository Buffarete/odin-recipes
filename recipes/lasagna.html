Lasagna is a hearty Italian casserole layered with rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, tender pasta sheets, and plenty of melted cheese on top. This comforting dish is perfect for family dinners or special occasions, offering a wonderful combination of flavors and textures in every bite. Slow-cooked meat sauce infused with garlic, tomatoes, and herbs forms the savory base, while layers of pasta and creamy cheese bring the dish’s structure and indulgence. Baked until bubbly and golden, lasagna is the ultimate soul-warming Italian classic.


Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g (1 lb) ground beef or a mix of beef and pork
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g (28 oz) canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 450g (16 oz) ricotta cheese or béchamel sauce
  • 250g (9 oz) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 50g (½ cup) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles in salted boiling water according to package instructions until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic, cooking until softened.
  4. Add the ground meat, breaking it apart with a spoon, and cook until browned.
  5. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  6. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce.
  7. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce, followed by a layer of ricotta or béchamel cheese, then sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Repeat layering: sauce, noodles, cheese mixture, and shredded cheese until all ingredients are used, finishing with a top layer of mozzarella and Parmesan.
  9. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  10. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes until the top is golden and bubbly.
  11. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving to allow it to set.
